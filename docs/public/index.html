HOW IT WORKS

Three steps.
That's it.

No accounts, no setup, no learning curve. If you can scan a QR code, you can use Beam.

STEP 01
💻

Open Beam on your PC

Download and install Beam. It starts a local server on your WiFi network automatically the moment you open it.

STEP 02
📱

Scan the QR code

Click QR Connect. Point your phone camera at the code. Your phone browser opens Beam instantly — no app needed.

STEP 03
⚡

Start transferring

Send files, stream videos, or beam clipboard text between any connected devices at full local WiFi speed.

ON CLOSE
🔒

Everything is deleted

When you close Beam, all files are permanently wiped. Nothing stays on your machine after a session ends.

FEATURES

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

Built lean. Every feature solves a real problem. Nothing added for show.

📁

File Transfer

Send any file in any direction — PC to phone, phone to PC. Select multiple files and they queue automatically with per-file progress.

⚡

Beam Direct

Send a file straight to a specific connected device. They get an accept/decline popup and the file downloads immediately — no server involved.

📋

Clipboard Sync

Copy something on your phone, paste it into Beam, pick a device, and send it. Works for links, notes, passwords — anything text.

▶️

Stream Without Downloading

Videos play directly in the browser. Images preview inline. PDFs and text render on screen. No download required to view.

📲

QR Code Connect

One scan and your phone is connected. No typing IP addresses, no pairing codes, no app installation. Just your phone camera.

🔒

Zero Data Retention

All files deleted automatically when you close Beam. No data ever leaves your local network. No accounts, no cloud, no telemetry.

🖥️

Live Device List

See every device connected to your session in real time. Auto-identified as iPhone, Android, Windows PC, Mac, and more.

🔄

Auto Updates

Beam checks for new versions on launch and notifies you automatically. No manual reinstalling ever needed.
